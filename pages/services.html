Our Services

Expert fabrication and installation of architectural aluminium and glass systems.

What We Do

Fabrication & Installation Specialists

We work exclusively as a subcontractor, focusing on precision fabrication and expert installation for main aluminium contractors.

Important: We do not provide design, consulting, or project quotation services. Materials are supplied by the main contractor. We focus solely on fabrication and installation excellence.

Curtain Wall Systems

We fabricate and install both stick and unitized curtain wall systems for commercial and residential high-rise buildings. Our team works with all major international systems including Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, AluK, and Guttmann.

  • Stick curtain wall fabrication
  • Unitized curtain wall assembly
  • Structural glazing systems
  • Point-fixed glazing

ACP Cladding

Aluminium Composite Panel cladding systems for modern building exteriors. We handle fabrication, cutting, bending, and installation with precision to achieve seamless facade finishes.

  • Panel cutting and shaping
  • Cassette fabrication
  • Subframe installation
  • Weather-sealed joints

Skylights

Architectural skylight systems that bring natural light into interior spaces while maintaining weatherproofing and thermal performance. From simple flat skylights to complex pyramidal structures.

  • Flat skylight systems
  • Pyramidal skylights
  • Barrel vault designs
  • Operable skylights

Doors & Windows

High-performance aluminium door and window systems for commercial and residential applications. We fabricate and install a wide range of configurations for any project requirement.

  • Swing doors & windows
  • Sliding systems
  • Folding doors
  • Pivot systems

Glass Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless glass railing systems for balconies, staircases, and terraces. Our installations combine safety compliance with elegant aesthetics.

  • Frameless systems
  • Semi-frameless railings
  • Handrail integration
  • Custom configurations

Automatic Doors

Sensor-operated automatic door systems for commercial entrances, providing convenience and accessibility while maintaining security and energy efficiency.

  • Sliding automatic doors
  • Revolving doors
  • Swing automatic systems
  • Access control integration

Rolling Shutters

Aluminium rolling shutter systems for security, privacy, and climate control. Available in manual and motorized configurations for retail, industrial, and residential applications.

  • Security shutters
  • Perforated designs
  • Motorized systems
  • Fire-rated options
