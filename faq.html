Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our aluminium facade fabrication, installation services, and partnership process.

General

What does Madinat Al Saada do?
Madinat Al Saada Aluminium & Glass Works LLC specializes in the fabrication and installation of aluminium facade systems. We work on curtain walls, ACP cladding, skylights, doors & windows, glass balustrades, automatic doors, and rolling shutters for construction projects across the UAE.
Do you work directly with end clients?
No. We operate exclusively as a subcontractor for main aluminium contractors and facade companies. Materials are supplied by the main contractor — we provide the skilled labor, factory fabrication, and on-site installation. This model allows us to focus entirely on precision workmanship.
Where is your facility located?
Our factory and office are located on Amman Street in Industrial Area 1, Ajman, United Arab Emirates. We serve projects across all Emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ras Al Khaimah, and the Northern Emirates.
How long has the company been in business?
Madinat Al Saada was established in 2016. Over the past decade, we have completed 17+ major projects including high-rise towers, commercial developments, and villa communities across the UAE, building a reputation for reliable, high-quality facade work.

Services

What aluminium systems do you work with?
We are proficient with all major international aluminium systems including Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, AluK, Guttmann, Hydro, Hueck, Alumil, UCS, and others. We do not work with generic or unbranded profiles — only premium systems engineered for performance.
Do you supply the materials?
No. As a subcontractor, materials (aluminium profiles, glass, hardware, sealants) are supplied by the main contractor. We receive the materials at our factory, fabricate according to approved shop drawings, and install on site. This separation ensures clear accountability and cost transparency.
What is the difference between stick and unitized curtain walls?
Stick systems are assembled piece-by-piece on site — mullions, transoms, and glass installed individually. They suit low to mid-rise buildings and irregular geometries. Unitized systems are factory-assembled as complete panels, then crane-lifted and installed floor by floor. They are faster (up to 50% time savings), have better quality control, and are ideal for high-rise towers with repetitive floor plates.
Do you handle both fabrication and installation?
Yes. We are a full-service facade subcontractor. Our factory in Ajman handles all fabrication — cutting, machining, assembly, and quality checks. Our site crews then install the finished products across project locations in the UAE. This end-to-end approach ensures consistency from factory to facade.

Quality

What certifications do you hold?
We hold three ISO certifications: ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management System), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management System), and ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health & Safety Management System). These are independently audited and renewed annually.
How do you ensure quality during fabrication?
Our ISO 9001 quality management system governs every stage — incoming material inspection, CNC machining tolerances, assembly checks, and final QC before dispatch. We maintain detailed checklists, non-conformance reports, and traceability records for every project. All work follows approved shop drawings signed off by the main contractor's engineering team.
What equipment do you use?
Our factory houses 15+ specialized machines including double-head cutting saws, CNC machining centers, copy routers, crimping machines, corner cleaning machines, glass processing equipment, and sealant application systems. All equipment is regularly maintained and calibrated to manufacturer standards.

Partnership

How do we start working together?
Contact us with your project details — scope, system type, quantities, and timeline. We will review the requirements, visit the site if needed, and provide a competitive quotation within 48 hours. Once awarded, we assign a dedicated project manager and begin shop drawing coordination immediately.
What areas of the UAE do you cover?
We cover all seven Emirates. Our active project sites span Dubai (Marina, Business Bay, Creek Harbour, Dubai South), Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, and Ras Al Khaimah. Our central location in Ajman gives us efficient access to projects across the northern and central UAE.
What project sizes do you handle?
We handle projects ranging from villa communities and low-rise commercial buildings to 50+ floor high-rise towers. Our workforce of 90+ skilled workers across 10+ active sites allows us to manage multiple projects simultaneously without compromising quality or timelines.
