Quality Assurance

Quality Control

Four-stage inspection process ensuring every component meets ISO 9001 standards before leaving the factory.

01

Material Inspection

Incoming aluminium profiles and glass panels verified against mill certificates, alloy grades, and dimensional tolerances before entering production.

02

In-Process Check

Random sampling at each production stage — cut lengths, drill positions, and crimp pressure verified against shop drawings.

03

Dimensional Accuracy

Finished frames and panels measured with calibrated instruments — diagonal checks, squareness, and gasket groove depths confirmed.

04

Final Inspection

Complete unit assembly trial, hardware function test, and visual surface inspection before protective wrapping and dispatch to site.
