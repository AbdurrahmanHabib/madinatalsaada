About Madinat Al Saada

Your trusted partner for precision aluminium fabrication and installation since 2016.

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2016

Madinat Al Saada Aluminium & Glass Works LLC was established in 2016 with a clear mission: to become the most trusted subcontractor for aluminium fabrication and installation in the UAE.

We work exclusively with leading main contractors, providing them with precision fabrication and expert installation services. Our focus on quality over quantity has earned us long-term partnerships with major players in the industry.

Unlike full-service contractors, we specialize purely in fabrication and installation. This focused approach allows us to perfect our craft and deliver exceptional results on every project.
