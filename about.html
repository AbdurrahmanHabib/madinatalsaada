About Madinat Al Saada

Your trusted partner for precision aluminium fabrication and installation since 2016.

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2016

Madinat Al Saada Aluminium & Glass Works LLC was established in 2016 with a clear vision: to become the most reliable subcontractor for aluminium and glass fabrication and installation in the UAE. What started as a small workshop in Ajman has grown into a fully equipped facility serving major construction projects across every emirate.

We work exclusively with leading main contractors, providing them with precision fabrication and expert installation services for high-rise towers, luxury villas, hospitality developments, and commercial buildings. Our partnerships with companies like National Aluminium LLC and Al Qassim Aluminium & Glass are built on years of consistent quality and on-time delivery.

Unlike full-service contractors who spread their resources across design, procurement, and project management, we focus purely on fabrication and installation. This specialization allows us to invest deeply in our machinery, train our workforce to the highest standards, and deliver results that exceed expectations on every project we undertake.
