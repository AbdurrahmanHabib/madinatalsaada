Who We Are

Company Overview

Established in 2016 in Ajman, UAE, Madinat Al Saada Aluminium & Glass Works LLC is a specialist aluminium fabrication and installation company operating from a dedicated 2,500 sqm production facility in Industrial Area 1.

With a team of 109 professionals — including 50 site installation staff, 47 factory workers, 3 QHSE officers, and 9 office staff — we deliver end-to-end aluminium and glazing solutions for commercial towers, residential communities, hospitality, and mixed-use developments across all Emirates.

We have built our reputation as both an independent specialist and a trusted execution partner for tier-1 facade contractors including National Aluminium LLC (GINCO Group), Prime Metal Industries, and Al Qassim Aluminium & Glass.

Our factory houses 17 specialized machines — Emmegi CNC systems, FOM saws, SETH drilling centers, and Pressta crimping machines — enabling precision manufacturing of curtain walls, ACP cladding, skylights, doors, windows, glass balustrades, and rolling shutters.

QHSE Policy

Quality, Health, Safety & Environment

We are committed to the highest standards of quality management, occupational safety, and environmental responsibility across all our operations.

Safe Working Conditions

We maintain safe working conditions across all factory operations and construction sites. Mandatory PPE, toolbox talks before every shift, and strict working-at-heights protocols ensure the safety of every team member.

Quality Management

ISO 9001 certified quality management system. Every project undergoes rigorous inspection at material receipt, during fabrication, at assembly, and before delivery to site.

Occupational Health

ISO 45001 certified occupational health and safety management. Regular health checks, safety training, and incident reporting protocols protect our workforce on every project.

Environmental Responsibility

ISO 14001 certified environmental management. We recycle aluminium offcuts, minimize waste, and follow sustainable practices throughout our fabrication and installation processes.
