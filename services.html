01 — SERVICE

Curtain Wall Systems

We fabricate and install both unitized and stick curtain wall systems for commercial towers, mixed-use developments, and high-rise residential buildings. Our in-house CNC fabrication ensures precision-fit assemblies that meet exacting tolerances.

  • Unitized curtain wall systems
  • Stick curtain wall systems
  • Structural glazing with silicone joints
  • Thermal break aluminium profiles
02 — SERVICE

ACP Cladding

Aluminium composite panel cladding systems with concealed and exposed fix options. We handle everything from panel grooving and folding in our factory to full on-site installation including sub-structure framing.

  • Concealed fix cassette systems
  • Exposed riveted systems
  • Fire-rated FR core panels
  • Custom colour and finish options
03 — SERVICE

Skylights

Structural glass roof systems designed for natural light and architectural impact. We engineer and install pyramidal, ridge, barrel vault, and custom-geometry skylight systems for atriums, lobbies, and public spaces.

  • Pyramidal and ridge skylights
  • Barrel vault systems
  • Custom geometry configurations
  • Laminated safety glass options
04 — SERVICE

Doors & Windows

Full range of aluminium door and window systems including hinged, turn-and-tilt, sliding, and folding configurations. All systems available with thermal break profiles for energy efficiency and acoustic performance.

  • Hinged and turn-and-tilt windows
  • Sliding windows and doors
  • Bi-fold and lift-and-slide systems
  • Thermal break profiles for insulation
05 — SERVICE

Glass Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless glass balustrade systems for balconies, terraces, staircases, and podium edges. We supply and install complete systems including base channels, standoff fixings, and handrails.

  • Frameless channel systems
  • Standoff point-fixed balustrades
  • Semi-frameless post systems
  • Tempered and laminated safety glass
06 — SERVICE

Rolling Shutters

Motorized and manual aluminium rolling shutter systems for commercial and residential applications. Providing security, privacy, climate control, and storm protection with clean aesthetic integration.

  • Motorized automatic operation
  • Manual spring-loaded systems
  • Perforated slat options
  • Powder-coated aluminium finishes
