Facade Engineering

Curtain Wall Comparison

Understanding the right facade system for your project — from traditional stick to advanced unitized construction.

Traditional

Stick System

Site-assembled facade system where individual mullions, transoms, and glass panels are installed piece by piece on the building.

Advantages

  • Lower upfront material cost
  • Flexible for irregular geometries
  • Easier to modify during construction
  • Suitable for low to mid-rise buildings
  • Simpler logistics and transport

Best Applications

  • Villas and low-rise commercial
  • Retail facades and shopfronts
  • Renovation and retrofit projects
  • Buildings under 15 floors
Advanced

Unitized System

Factory-assembled facade panels shipped as complete units and installed floor-by-floor using crane lifting — minimal on-site labor.

Advantages

  • Faster installation (50% time savings)
  • Superior factory quality control
  • Better weather sealing performance
  • Reduced on-site labor requirements
  • Ideal for high-rise with tight schedules

Best Applications

  • High-rise towers (15+ floors)
  • Commercial office towers
  • Projects with accelerated timelines
  • Repetitive floor plate designs
Glass Technology

Glass Specifications

We work with every type of architectural glass to meet structural, thermal, acoustic, and aesthetic requirements.

◊

Tempered

Heat-strengthened safety glass, 4-5x stronger than annealed. Shatters into small granular chunks on impact.

◇

Laminated

Two glass layers bonded with PVB interlayer. Holds together on breakage — ideal for overhead and balustrade use.

▣

Insulated DGU

Double-glazed units with sealed air or argon gap. Reduces heat gain by up to 50% — essential for UAE climate.

◐

Low-E Coated

Microscopically thin metallic coating reflects infrared heat while allowing visible light transmission.

▧

Tinted

Body-tinted glass (grey, bronze, green, blue) reduces solar heat gain and glare while maintaining views.

◉

Reflective

Metallic oxide coating provides mirror-like exterior appearance and superior solar control performance.

▤

Frosted

Acid-etched or sandblasted for privacy while diffusing natural light. Used in partitions and bathroom screens.

◆

Fire-Rated

Intumescent interlayer glass providing 30-120 minutes fire resistance. Required for stairwells and escape corridors.
