Our Services

End-to-end aluminium and glass facade solutions — fabricated in our own factory, installed by our own crews.

What We Do

Fabrication & Installation

We fabricate and install seven categories of aluminium and glass systems for the UAE's leading main contractors. All work is performed in-house from our Ajman factory and deployed by our own site teams.

Important Notice

Madinat Al Saada operates exclusively as a B2B subcontractor. We work only with main aluminium and glass contractors—not directly with developers, consultants, or end clients. Materials and profile systems are supplied by the main contractor.

01

Curtain Wall Systems

High-performance unitized and stick-system curtain wall facades for commercial towers, residential high-rises, and mixed-use developments. We handle full fabrication, assembly, and on-site installation.

Unitized panel fabrication with factory-sealed joints
Stick-system assembly for complex geometries
Structural silicone glazing and pressure-plate systems
Compatible with Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, and AluK profiles
02

ACP Cladding

Aluminium Composite Panel cladding systems with concealed fix framing for clean, contemporary building envelopes. Precision-routed and folded in our factory for seamless on-site assembly.

Concealed-fix and riveted cassette systems
CNC routing and folding for exact panel geometry
Fire-rated A2 core panels available
Custom colour matching and finish coordination
03

Skylights

Structural glass roof systems that bring natural light into lobbies, atriums, and public spaces. Engineered for thermal performance, waterproofing, and wind load resistance in the UAE climate.

Pyramid, ridge, barrel vault, and flat-glass configurations
Thermally broken aluminium rafter systems
Laminated and insulated glass options
Integrated drainage and condensation management
04

Doors & Windows

Complete door and window fabrication across all standard and premium configurations. Thermal break profiles with multi-point locking for security, sound insulation, and energy performance.

Casement, sliding, tilt-and-turn, and folding systems
Thermal break profiles for energy efficiency
Multi-point locking and hardware installation
Large-format sliding doors up to 3m height
05

Glass Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless glass balustrade systems for balconies, staircases, podium edges, and pool surrounds. Engineered for safety and designed for minimal visual obstruction.

Frameless channel-set and point-fixed systems
Semi-frameless with aluminium or stainless steel posts
Tempered and laminated safety glass
Handrail integration in round and rectangular profiles
06

Automatic Doors

Motorized sliding, swing, and revolving door systems for hotels, commercial buildings, and retail spaces. Complete installation including operators, sensors, and safety compliance.

Sliding, swing, and telescopic automatic operators
Motion sensors and safety beam integration
Fire-rated and emergency breakout configurations
Maintenance and service support
07

Rolling Shutters

Motorized and manual aluminium rolling shutters for warehouses, retail units, parking entries, and residential applications. Built for durability, security, and smooth daily operation.

Motorized tubular motor and chain-drive options
Perforated, solid, and vision-panel slat profiles
Wind-lock guides for high-wind exposure
Remote control and smart-home integration
How It Works

Our Process

From shop drawings to handover, a clear five-step workflow that keeps every project on track.

Shop Drawings

Detailed fabrication drawings prepared from consultant-approved architectural plans.

Material Coordination

Profile systems and glass specifications confirmed with the main contractor.

Factory Fabrication

CNC cutting, machining, assembly, and quality inspection in our Ajman facility.

Site Installation

Our crews install all systems on-site with full safety compliance and coordination.

Handover & Snag

Final inspection, snag clearance, and handover documentation to close the project.
