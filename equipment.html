Factory Overview

2,500 SQM Dedicated Production Facility

Our factory in Industrial Area 1, Ajman is equipped with 17 specialized machines from leading European and international manufacturers — Emmegi, FOM Industrie, Thomas, Reinhardt-Technik, and SETH.

From CNC 4-axis machining and double-head cutting to corner crimping, composite panel routing, and silicon dispensing — every stage of aluminium fabrication is handled in-house with precision and quality control.
