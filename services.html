Our Services

Expert fabrication and installation of architectural aluminium and glass systems.

What We Do

Fabrication & Installation Specialists

We work exclusively as a subcontractor, focusing on precision fabrication and expert installation for main aluminium contractors.

Important: We do not provide design, consulting, or project quotation services. Materials are supplied by the main contractor. We focus solely on fabrication and installation excellence.

Curtain Wall Systems

We fabricate and install both stick and unitized curtain wall systems for commercial and residential high-rise buildings. Our team works with all major international systems including Technal, Reynaers, AluK, and Guttmann, delivering precision-assembled facades that meet the most demanding architectural specifications.

  • Stick curtain wall fabrication
  • Unitized curtain wall assembly
  • Structural glazing systems
  • Point-fixed glazing

ACP Cladding

Aluminium Composite Panel cladding systems transform building exteriors with clean, modern aesthetics. We handle the full fabrication process -- panel cutting, bending, cassette assembly -- followed by professional on-site installation with weather-sealed joints and precise subframe alignment for a seamless finished facade.

  • Panel cutting and shaping
  • Cassette fabrication
  • Subframe installation
  • Weather-sealed joints

Skylights

Architectural skylight systems bring natural light into lobbies, atriums, and common areas while maintaining weatherproofing and thermal performance. From simple flat configurations to complex pyramidal and barrel vault structures, we fabricate and install skylight systems that combine structural integrity with elegant design.

  • Flat skylight systems
  • Pyramidal skylights
  • Barrel vault designs
  • Operable skylights

Doors & Windows

High-performance aluminium door and window systems for commercial towers, residential developments, and hospitality projects. We fabricate and install a wide range of configurations -- from standard swing and sliding units to premium folding and pivot systems -- all engineered for thermal efficiency and long-term durability in the UAE climate.

  • Swing doors and windows
  • Sliding systems
  • Folding doors
  • Pivot systems

Glass Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless glass railing systems for balconies, staircases, pool areas, and terraces. Our installations combine strict safety compliance with elegant, unobstructed views. Every balustrade is fabricated to exact measurements and installed with precision-machined fittings for a flawless finish.

  • Frameless glass systems
  • Semi-frameless railings
  • Handrail integration
  • Custom configurations

Automatic Doors

Sensor-operated automatic door systems for commercial entrances, hotel lobbies, retail storefronts, and office buildings. We install sliding, revolving, and swing automatic door units that provide seamless accessibility while maintaining security, energy efficiency, and a premium aesthetic at every entrance point.

  • Sliding automatic doors
  • Revolving doors
  • Swing automatic systems
  • Access control integration

Rolling Shutters

Aluminium rolling shutter systems for security, privacy, and climate control across retail, industrial, and residential applications. Available in manual and motorized configurations with options for perforated visibility, fire-rated protection, and remote-controlled operation to suit any commercial or residential requirement.

  • Security shutters
  • Perforated designs
  • Motorized systems
  • Fire-rated options
