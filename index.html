About Us

We Believe That Every Facade Is the Signature of a Building.

Established in 2016 in Ajman, UAE, Madinat Al Saada is a specialist aluminium fabrication and installation company with a decade of experience in the UAE facade industry. Operating from our dedicated 2,500 sqm production facility, we deliver end-to-end aluminium and glazing solutions.

We have built our reputation as both an independent specialist and a trusted execution partner for tier-1 facade contractors including National Aluminium LLC, Prime Metal Industries, and Al Qassim Aluminium & Glass.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?
We provide complete facade solutions including curtain walls (unitized and stick), ACP cladding, skylights, doors & windows, glass balustrades, and rolling shutters — from fabrication through to installation.
How does the project process work?
We handle the full facade lifecycle — shop drawings, material procurement, CNC fabrication in our Ajman factory, quality inspection, delivery, and on-site installation by our own trained crews across all Emirates.
Where is your factory?
Our 2,500 sqm factory is in Industrial Area 1, Ajman, UAE. We serve projects across all Emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and the Northern Emirates.
Which aluminium systems do you work with?
We have hands-on experience with Schüco, Technal, AluK, Alumil, Gutmann, Gulf Extrusion, and all locally available aluminium systems in the UAE market.
Do you handle both fabrication and installation?
Yes. We operate our own factory with 17 specialized machines and deploy our own 50+ trained installation crews to site. We are a complete facade provider — not a subcontractor.
