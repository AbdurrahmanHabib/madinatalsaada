FAQ

Common Questions

What services do you provide?
We specialize in fabrication and installation of curtain walls, ACP cladding, skylights, doors & windows, glass balustrades, automatic doors, and rolling shutters.
Do you work with end clients?
No. We operate exclusively as a subcontractor for main aluminium contractors. Materials are supplied by the main contractor.
What systems do you work with?
Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, AluK, Guttmann, and other premium international systems. We do not work with generic profiles.
Where is your factory?
Industrial Area 1, Ajman, UAE. We serve projects across all Emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and the Northern Emirates.
What certifications do you hold?
ISO 9001:2015 (Quality), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental), and ISO 45001:2018 (Health & Safety).
