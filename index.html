Our Expertise

Fabrication & Installation Services

End-to-end aluminium and glass solutions — fabricated in-house, installed by our own crews.

Curtain Walls

Unitized and stick-system facades for high-rise towers. Engineered for structural performance and weather tightness.

ACP Cladding

Aluminium composite panel systems for modern facades. Precise fabrication with concealed fix for clean finishes.

Skylights

Structural glass roof systems for maximum daylight. Custom geometries including pyramids and barrel vaults.

Doors & Windows

Glass Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless systems for balconies and podiums. Engineered to UAE building codes.

Rolling Shutters

Motorized and manual aluminium shutters for storefronts and warehouses. Powder-coated with safety features.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Madinat Al Saada provide?
We specialize in fabrication and installation of aluminium and glass systems including curtain walls, ACP cladding, skylights, doors, windows, glass balustrades, automatic doors, and rolling shutters.
Do you work directly with end clients?
No. We operate exclusively as a subcontractor working with main aluminium contractors. Materials are supplied by the main contractor. We focus on fabrication and installation.
What aluminium systems do you work with?
We work exclusively with international brands: Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, AluK, Guttmann, and other premium systems. We do not work with generic or low-quality profiles.
Where is your factory?
Our 15,000 sq ft fabrication facility is in Industrial Area 1, Ajman, UAE. We serve projects across all Emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and the Northern Emirates.
What certifications do you hold?
We hold ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management), and ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health & Safety).
