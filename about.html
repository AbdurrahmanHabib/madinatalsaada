About Madinat Al Saada

A decade of precision aluminium facade fabrication and installation, built on craft, discipline, and international-grade systems.

Our Story

Built on Precision, Grown Through Trust

Madinat Al Saada Aluminium & Glass Works LLC was founded in 2016 in Ajman, UAE, with a clear vision: to deliver international-quality aluminium facade fabrication and installation as a dedicated subcontractor.

From our purpose-built factory in Industrial Area 1, we serve the UAE's leading aluminium main contractors. We do not work with end clients or developers directly—this focused model allows us to invest entirely in craft, equipment, and workforce quality.

Today, we operate across 10+ active project sites simultaneously, with 90+ skilled workers and a fleet of 15 CNC and fabrication machines from Emmegi, Rinaldi, and Pressta Eisele.

Our Approach

How We Work

Three principles that define every project we take on.

01

Focused Expertise

We do one thing and do it exceptionally: aluminium facade fabrication and installation. No diversification, no dilution. Every resource is dedicated to our craft.

02

Trusted Partnerships

We work exclusively with international-grade profile systems—Technal, Schüco, Reynaers—and partner only with established main contractors who share our standards.

With 90+ workers deployed across 10+ sites simultaneously, we maintain strict scheduling discipline. Our factory runs continuously to meet project timelines without compromise.
