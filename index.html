Our Expertise

Fabrication & Installation Services

We deliver end-to-end aluminium and glass solutions for commercial, residential, and mixed-use developments across the UAE. Every system is fabricated in-house and installed by our own trained crews.

Curtain Walls

Unitized and stick-system curtain wall facades for high-rise towers and commercial buildings. Engineered for structural performance, weather tightness, and aesthetic precision.

ACP Cladding

Aluminium composite panel cladding for exterior facades and interior feature walls. Precise fabrication with concealed fix or rivet systems for clean, modern finishes.

Skylights

Structural glass skylights and roof glazing systems designed for maximum daylight with minimal thermal gain. Custom geometries including pyramids, ridges, and barrel vaults.

Doors & Windows

Casement, sliding, tilt-and-turn, and folding aluminium door and window systems. Thermally broken profiles for energy efficiency with premium hardware options.

Glass Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless glass balustrade systems for balconies, staircases, and podium edges. Engineered to meet UAE building codes with laminated safety glass.

Rolling Shutters

Motorized and manual aluminium rolling shutters for storefronts, warehouses, and parking entrances. Powder-coated finishes with integrated safety features.

How We Work

Our 5-Step Process

1

Order Receipt

We receive your specifications, BOQ, and architectural drawings. Our estimation team reviews every detail and provides a competitive quotation within 48 hours.

2

Shop Drawings

Our CAD team prepares detailed shop drawings with precise measurements, system selections, and hardware specifications. All drawings are submitted for consultant approval before fabrication begins.

3

Fabrication

Profiles are cut, machined, and assembled in our 15,000 sq ft Ajman factory using CNC routers, double-head saws, and crimping machines. Every unit is assembled to millimetre precision.

4

Quality Check

Each fabricated unit undergoes a multi-point inspection covering dimensional accuracy, hardware function, sealant application, and finish quality before dispatch to site.

5

Installation

Our experienced installation crews handle site delivery, anchoring, glazing, sealing, and final commissioning. We coordinate directly with the main contractor to maintain project timelines.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Madinat Al Saada provide?

We specialize in the fabrication and installation of aluminium and glass building envelopes. Our core services include curtain wall systems, ACP cladding, skylights and roof glazing, aluminium doors and windows, glass balustrades, and rolling shutters. We handle the full cycle from shop drawings through factory fabrication to on-site installation.

Do you work directly with end clients or property owners?

No. We operate exclusively as a B2B subcontractor. We partner with main contractors, aluminium trading companies, and construction firms who engage us for fabrication and installation on their projects. If you are a homeowner or end user, we recommend contacting one of our partner companies who can coordinate the full scope including supply and installation.

What aluminium systems do you fabricate?

We are experienced with all major international aluminium profile systems including Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, AluK, and Guttmann. We also work with local extrusion suppliers for bespoke profiles. Our factory is equipped to handle unitized curtain walls, stick-system facades, and standard door and window assemblies across all these brands.

Where is your factory located?

Our fabrication facility is located on Amman Street in Industrial Area 1, Ajman, UAE. The 15,000 sq ft factory houses CNC machinery, double-head cutting saws, copy routers, crimping machines, and a dedicated quality inspection area. We serve projects across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, and the wider UAE.

What quality certifications do you hold?

We maintain three ISO certifications: ISO 9001:2015 for quality management, ISO 14001:2015 for environmental management, and ISO 45001:2018 for occupational health and safety. These certifications are audited annually and cover our factory operations, site installations, and management processes.
