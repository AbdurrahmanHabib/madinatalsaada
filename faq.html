Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and how we work.

General

What does Madinat Al Saada do?

We specialise in the fabrication and installation of aluminium and glass systems for the construction industry. Our scope covers curtain walls, ACP cladding, skylights, aluminium doors and windows, glass balustrades, automatic doors, and rolling shutters. We handle the full cycle from shop drawings and factory fabrication to on-site installation and commissioning.

Do you work directly with end clients?

No. We operate exclusively as a B2B subcontractor. We partner with main contractors, aluminium trading companies, and construction firms who engage us for fabrication and installation on their projects. If you are a homeowner or property developer, we recommend contacting one of our partner contractors who can coordinate the full supply-and-install scope.

Where is your facility located?

Our fabrication facility is located on Amman Street in Industrial Area 1, Ajman, UAE. The 15,000 sq ft factory houses CNC machinery, double-head cutting saws, copy routers, crimping machines, and a dedicated quality inspection area. We serve projects across all Emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Umm Al Quwain, and Fujairah.

How long have you been in business?

Madinat Al Saada Aluminium & Glass Works LLC was founded in 2016. With 10 years of experience and over 17 completed projects, we have built a strong track record delivering aluminium and glass installations for residential towers, commercial buildings, and mixed-use developments ranging from villa clusters to 39-floor high-rise towers.

Services

What aluminium systems do you work with?

We are experienced with all major international aluminium profile systems including Technal, Schüco, Reynaers, AluK, and Guttmann. We also work with local extrusion suppliers for bespoke profiles. Our factory is equipped to handle unitized curtain walls, stick-system facades, and standard door and window assemblies across all these brands.

Do you supply materials?

No. Materials—including aluminium profiles, glass, hardware, and sealants—are supplied by the main contractor or their nominated suppliers. We focus exclusively on fabrication and installation, which allows us to maintain competitive pricing and concentrate on what we do best: precision manufacturing and expert site work.

What is the difference between stick and unitized curtain walls?

Stick systems are assembled piece-by-piece on site: mullions and transoms are installed first, then glazing panels are added individually. They are more cost-effective and flexible for complex geometries but slower to install. Unitized systems are pre-assembled in our factory as complete panels with glass already fitted, then lifted and connected on site. They offer faster installation, better quality control, and are ideal for high-rise buildings where speed and weather-tightness are critical.

Do you handle both fabrication and installation?

Yes. We provide a complete fabrication-and-installation service. Aluminium profiles are cut, machined, and assembled in our Ajman factory, then transported to site where our trained installation crews handle anchoring, glazing, sealing, and final commissioning. This integrated approach ensures quality consistency from factory floor to finished facade.

Quality

What certifications do you hold?

We maintain three internationally recognised ISO certifications: ISO 9001:2015 for quality management, ISO 14001:2015 for environmental management, and ISO 45001:2018 for occupational health and safety. These certifications are audited annually and cover our factory operations, site installations, and management processes.

How do you ensure quality?

We follow a 4-step quality control process on every project: (1) incoming material inspection to verify profiles, glass, and hardware meet specifications; (2) in-process checks during cutting, machining, and assembly; (3) dimensional verification of completed units against shop drawings; and (4) final inspection and sign-off before dispatch to site. On site, our supervisors conduct daily installation quality audits.

What equipment do you use?

Our 15,000 sq ft factory is equipped with 15 specialised machines including CNC routers for precision machining, double-head cutting saws for accurate profile cutting, copy routers, single-head saws, crimping machines, corner cleaning machines, glass processing equipment, and drilling machines. This comprehensive equipment lineup allows us to handle all major aluminium profile systems in-house.
