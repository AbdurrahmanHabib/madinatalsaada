SYSTEMS WE Work With

Direct fabrication and installation experience with six internationally recognized aluminium system providers.

Germany

Schuco

FWS Series (Curtain Walls) · ASS Series (Sliding)

World-leading German system provider for high-performance curtain walls and sliding door systems. Known for thermal efficiency, structural integrity, and innovative engineering across commercial and residential applications.

France · Hydro Group

Technal

Curtain Wall · Window · Door Systems

French aluminium systems by Hydro, widely specified across the Middle East for curtain walls, windows, and doors. Proven track record in high-rise and commercial facade applications with comprehensive technical support.

Italy

AluK

58BW Series · GT55 Series

Italian-engineered aluminium systems offering premium window, door, and curtain wall solutions. The 58BW and GT55 series deliver precision engineering with excellent thermal break performance for UAE climate conditions.

Greece

Alumil

M11000 Series · S77 Series

Greek manufacturer providing a comprehensive range of architectural aluminium systems. The M11000 curtain wall and S77 sliding series are engineered for large-scale commercial and residential facade projects.

Germany · Est. 1937

Gutmann

Curtain Wall · Window · Door Systems

German aluminium systems manufacturer with over 85 years of engineering heritage. Known for precision-engineered profiles and innovative facade solutions for demanding architectural applications.

United Arab Emirates

Gulf Extrusion

Local Extrusion · Custom Profiles

UAE-based aluminium extrusion manufacturer providing locally produced profiles optimized for the regional market. Fast lead times, custom die capabilities, and competitive pricing for UAE facade projects.
